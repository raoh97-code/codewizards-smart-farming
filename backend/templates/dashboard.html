Soil guide

Quick reference for pH, NPK roles, and micronutrients — use alongside your results.

pH scale

AcidNeutralAlkaline

Most field crops prefer roughly 6.0–7.2. Adjust liming or sulfur based on tests — SmartFarm surfaces your actual pH in charts.

NPK roles

  • N — Leaf growth, biomass, chlorophyll
  • P — Roots, energy transfer, flowering
  • K — Stomata, disease stress, grain quality

Micronutrients at a glance

Element Typical concern Symptoms (broad)
Zn High pH soils Stunted growth, chlorosis
Fe Alkaline / wet Interveinal yellowing
Mn Sandy / high pH Necrotic spots on young leaves
